# Budget Buddy 💰

Budget Buddy is a user-friendly personal finance companion designed to simplify expense tracking and empower users to take control of their financial health. Whether you are saving for a milestone or just trying to manage daily expenses, Budget Buddy makes budgeting effortless.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Intuitive Expense Tracking:** Quickly log daily income and expenses with customizable categories (e.g., Food, Rent, Entertainment, Utilities).
- **Smart Budget Allocation:** Set monthly or weekly spending limits for different categories and receive alerts before you overspend.
- **Visual Analytics:** Interactive charts and graphs that break down your spending habits over time.
- **Financial Goal Setting:** Track your savings progress toward specific short-term or long-term goals.
